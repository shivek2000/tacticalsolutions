Who Are We?

Established in 2025, Tactical Force Security & Protection Services provides comprehensive and integrated security solutions built on the perfect balance of skilled manpower and advanced security technology. As an owner-managed business, our foundation is rooted in passion, accountability, and an in-depth understanding of South Africa’s diverse and complex security landscape. We recognised the growing need for a security provider that not only understands local challenges, but one that is agile enough to adapt, innovate, and deliver uncompromising protection in an ever-changing environment.

From the outset, our mission has been clear: to offer security services that uphold the highest standards of professionalism, reliability, and operational excellence. We believe that true security is proactive, not reactive—and this philosophy guides every aspect of our service delivery.

Our core offerings include professional guarding and rapid armed response, both executed by highly trained security officers equipped with the latest tools, communication systems, and tactical support. Whether deployed at residential estates, commercial properties, industrial sites, or high-risk environments, our teams are selected and trained to maintain a strong presence, respond swiftly, and mitigate threats before they escalate.
